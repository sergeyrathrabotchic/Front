Работа с текстом. Обсуждение. Ответы на вопросы.

Answer the questions about extracurricular activities.

1. Do you agree with the author’s opinion about extracurricular activities?

2. What arguments does the author give in favour of extracurricular activities?

3. Can you give more reasons to do after-school activities?

4. What are disadvantages of extracurricular  activities?

5. Discuss the disadvantages with you partner and make a list of them, e.g. 

…staying at school in the afternoon or even in the evening to play basketball or rehearse for a school play is very time-consuming.

Students usually feel tired after regular classes, moreover, they are to do a lot of homework.

As a result, after-school activities will contribute to more school overload.
